BEAST



Overview

Beast is an upcoming Indian Tamil-language action film written and directed by Nelson Dilipkumar and produced by Sun Pictures. The film stars Vijay and Pooja Hegde, with Selvaraghavan, Yogi Babu, Shine Tom Chacko, VTV Ganesh, Aparna Das, Lilliput Faruqui, Ankur Ajit Vikal and Sathish Krishnan in supporting roles. The film's music is composed by Anirudh Ravichander, whilst cinematography was handled by Manoj Paramahamsa and editing done by R. Nirmal, respectively. Beast is scheduled to release on January 2022, coinciding with Pongal.

Music

The music is composed by Anirudh Ravichander in his third collaboration with Vijay and Nelson.The album for Beast will reportedly have five songs and six theme tracks; three of the tracks were picturised on Vijay and Pooja Hegde, including a romantic single. Media reports had claimed that the introductory track in the Arabic music genre, which had vocals by Anirudh himself and features lyrics in Tamil and Arabic languages; it is considered to be released as the first single from the album.
